Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this topic until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time, waiting for feedback on each question before continuing. Asking multiple questions at once is bewildering.

If a topic is too broad guide me to narrow down into a better topic. Try to make each topic 10 lesson top.

Once reaching to an agreement use the skill /teach to create the new topic. Only create one topic at a time, NEVER multiple at once
